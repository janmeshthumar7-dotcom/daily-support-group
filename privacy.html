Privacy Policy

Daily Support Group Effective: 2026

Daily Support Group is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard personal information provided through our website and enquiries.

Personal information we collect

When you submit an enquiry, we may collect personal information including your name, phone number, email address, suburb or location, and details about the support you are requesting.

How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and communicate with you
  • To understand support needs and service availability
  • To arrange services, scheduling, and follow-up
  • For internal administration and record-keeping

Third-party processing (Formspree)

Our website enquiry form is delivered using Formspree, a third-party form processing service. When you submit a form, your information is transmitted securely to Formspree to deliver your enquiry to our email address.

Disclosure of information

We do not sell or trade personal information. Information is disclosed only where required to respond to enquiries, deliver services, comply with legal obligations, or where consent has been provided.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. While we use appropriate safeguards, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Access and correction

You may request access to or correction of personal information we hold about you by contacting us using the details below.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not intentionally collect sensitive information through cookies. If analytics or additional services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
